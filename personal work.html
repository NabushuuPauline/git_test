The love of God

His love is never ending

Once you get to know God personally, you begin to truly live. No one can love God or worship God on your behalf,they can pray for you but at the end of the day you need to also pray for yourself. There's a kind of joy and peace that surpasses human understanding, it can only come from the spirit of God. In the begining was the Word, the Word was with God and the Word was God.

God's unconditional love

God meets you from whichever point you are, He wont ask about where you have been or what took you so long, He marvels in the fact that you are there now, He marvel in your beauty, your scars, your uniqueness and just ny looking at you He knows exactly how to love and heal you.
